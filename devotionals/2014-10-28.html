Foreigners Included

Today's reading assignment is Ruth 1-4. As you read, pay attention to the fact that Ruth is not an Israelite and she is included in the people of God and the lineage of Jesus. As a book, it is a hope filled story in the midst of lots of battles and bloodshed.

Reflection Questions

Why would it be important for this story about a non-Israelite be included in the Bible?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

How does the message of Jesus in the New Testament reflect the themes seen in the story of Ruth in the Old Testament?

Additional Info

Moab was the land to the east of Judah beyond the Dead Sea.

New Testament Bonus

Read Galatians 3:23-29. How does the story of Ruth gives us a taste of what Jesus came to earth to do on behalf of all people?
