Week 1, Day 7

October 21, 2014

Freedom From Slavery

Today's reading assignment is Exodus 5:22-6:12, 11, and 12:1-42. As you read, pay attention to the boldness with which God speaks about the Israelites being led out of Egypt.

Reflection Questions

How is Pharaoh standing in the way of God’s plan? Participating in God’s plan?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

What does the boldness of God in delivering Israel tell you about God’s care for God’s people?

New Testament Bonus

Read John 1:29-34. How is the celebration of Passover reflected in Jesus’ life and death of the New Testament?
