Week 5, Day 6

November 17, 2014

Life of Exile: Brokenness

Today's reading assignment is Lamentations 3:1-24, Psalm 137, and Ezekiel 7. As you read, pay attention to the honest despair of a people who have seemingly lost their identity, their nation and their God.

Reflection Questions

What do the words of these verse tell us about how the Israelites felt about their relationship with God, both good and bad?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

How does grief, despair, and struggle form you spiritually, and what happens to your faith as a result?

New Testament Bonus

Read Matthew 26:69-75. Peter felt that he was left with nothing, how does his brokenness reflect that of the people of Judah after the exile?
