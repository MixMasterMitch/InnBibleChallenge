November 1, 2014

The Kingdom of David

Today's reading assignment is 2 Samuel 11-12:25 and 23:1-7. As you read, pay attention to: Even though under David, Israel becomes a powerful, prosperous kingdom, his life still demonstrates fallibility.

Reflection Questions

How do the events of David’s life make his role as God’s anointed more complicated to believe and understand?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

What actions in your life that you might have been ashamed of have you had to bring to God and ask for forgiveness?

New Testament Bonus

Read 1 John 1:5-10. What assurance do you have in your own faith that you are forgiven?
