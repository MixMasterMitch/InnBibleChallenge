A New Hope

Today's reading assignment is Matthew 1 and Luke 1:1-56. As you read, pay attention to the parts of Jesus story that are connected to the Old Testament stories.

The Persian Empire fell to the Greek Empire, and Israel was also overtaken and became a part of the Greek Empire, with a short period of independent rule by Israel. The next ruling empire is the Roman Empire, and at the start of the New Testament, Israel is ruled by King Herod, appointed by the Roman government.

Reflection Questions

How does Jesus fulfill the expectations for Israel for the Messiah?

Why do you think the Israelites might have been disappointed based on their expectations?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

How does the humble birth story of Jesus impact who you see Jesus as in your own life?
