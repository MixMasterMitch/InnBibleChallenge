Wisdom Literature: Ecclesiastes (attributed to King Solomon)

Today's reading assignment is Ecclesiastes 1:1-11, 2:1-3:22, 11:2-12:8. As you read, pay attention to the way Ecclesiastes emphasizes the pointlessness of the world and how many things remain unknown.

Reflection Questions
