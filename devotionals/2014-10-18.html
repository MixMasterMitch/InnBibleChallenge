Week 1, Day 4

October 18, 2014

Jacob & Family Become Israel

Today's reading assignment is Genesis 26:34-28:22 and 29:15-24. As you read, pay attention to the family dynamics which determine the future of Abraham’s offspring and the pain and jealousy determining everyone’s actions.

Reflection Questions

God speaks to Jacob in a dream. What do you notice about the promises made by God and those made by Jacob?

Connection to Your Faith

How do God’s promises allow you to avoid focusing on comparison?

New Testament Bonus

Read Matthew 10:1-4. Why do you think Jesus chose 12 as the number of disciples?
