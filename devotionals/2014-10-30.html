Saul, Israel’s First Monarch

Today's reading assignment is 1 Samuel 9-10:2, 10:17-27, 13:1-15, and 15. As you read, pay attention to how Saul’s disobedience and lack of humility gets in the way of leading God’s people in a way pleasing to God or to the people.

Reflection Questions

What characteristics did Saul possess that made him a seemingly logical choice for a king?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

In what ways has God moved you toward a more mature faith that you might have found yourself resisting and disobeying?

New Testament Bonus

Read Mark 10:35-45. How do Jesus’ teachings challenge the classic understanding of kingly leadership?
