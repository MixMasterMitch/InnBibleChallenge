Wisdom Literature: Proverbs (attributed to King Solomon)

Today's reading assignment is Proverbs 1, 10, and 14. As you read, pay attention to the proverbs as wisdom literature that is practical, what to consider when it comes to living as a person of faith in day to day life, and always choosing, “that which is better.”

Reflection Questions

How do the proverbs communicate God in a pragmatic, logical way?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

What are practical, simple ways your life reflects a faith in Jesus?

New Testament Bonus

Read Matthew 6:25-34. How is Jesus’ teaching like that of the Proverbs?
