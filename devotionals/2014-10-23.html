October 23, 2014

The Law: Part 1

Today's reading assignment is Leviticus 1, 9, 20, and 25:1-7. As you read, pay attention to the specifics of the laws given in order to draw closer to God.

Reflection Questions

How is a collection of legal requirements important to the story of God’s people becoming a community?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

Jesus said he came so the law could be fulfilled. How do you understand Jesus as the law fulfilled for your salvation?

New Testament Bonus

Read Matthew 5:17-20. Jesus’ challengers ask him for a “sign” to prove himself. What are the similarities with the Exodus rebellion?
