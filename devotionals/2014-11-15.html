November 15, 2014

Fall of Southern kingdom (Judah)

Today's reading assignment is 2 Kings 20:12-19, 2 Kings 23:1-25, Jeremiah 39. As you read, pay attention to the ways in which Judah claims to be obedient externally by doing all the right actions, yet its heart remains turned away from God.

Reflection Questions

What does it mean for the people of Judah that their story has suddenly come to an end?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

What does hope in Jesus offer us when it comes to trying to “keep the law” by actually obediently keeping the law?

New Testament Bonus

Read Matthew 19:16-27. In what ways is Jesus death an echo of the fall of Jerusalem?
