Week 1, Day 3

October 17, 2014

God of Promise

Today's reading assignment is Genesis 12:1-9, 15:1-21, 21:1-7, and 25:19-34. As you read, be sure to notice the promises about the future made to Abraham and God calling Abraham to make a covenant to be a blessing to the world.

Reflection Questions

What characteristics do you think Abraham possessed that drew God to make a covenant with him?

Connection to Your Faith

How are the promises made to Abraham fulfilled in the person of Jesus?

New Testament Bonus

Read Matthew 1:1-2, 1:17. Why do you think they included the genealogy of Jesus at the beginning of the New Testament?
