Week 4, Day 4

November 8, 2014

Back to the story, Israel Divided into Two Kingdoms

Today's reading assignment is 1 Kings 11:9-12:33. As you read, pay attention to Israel’s kings losing sight of their role as a people called by God for the sake of the world, while God mobilizes a community of resistance.

Reflection Questions

How is God involved in the public life of the nation of Israel? What does that say about God’s involvement in the world?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

The lamp is a symbol of God’s presence among the rebellion. How is this presence extended to the New Testament with Jesus and Jesus’ followers?

New Testament Bonus

Read Revelation 21:22-27. What does the New Heaven and New Earth eliminate our need of in this world?
