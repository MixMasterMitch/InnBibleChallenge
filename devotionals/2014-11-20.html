November 20, 2014

Life of Exile: Becoming Part of the Community

Today's reading assignment is Esther 2-4 and 7-8. As you read, pay attention to the experience of Israelites actually becoming a part of the foreign culture, and the positive impact that can result.

The Babylonian empire is overtaken by the Persian empire.

Reflection Questions

The book of Esther never once mentions “God.” Why do you think it’s still included in the Bible?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

When has community served as the spiritual experience you needed to be assured of your own faith?

New Testament Bonus

Read Luke 10:38-42. The Gospels make a point of talking about the way Jesus engaged with and taught women. How does Jesus turn the social norms upside down?
