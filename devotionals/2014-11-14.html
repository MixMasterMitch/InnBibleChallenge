Week 5, Day 3

November 14, 2014

Southern Prophets

Today's reading assignment is Jeremiah 1:1-19, Habakkuk 1:1-2:1, and Jeremiah 7:1-20. As you read, pay attention to the violence and brutality proclaimed by the prophets who had already seen destruction and were warning Judah of more to come.

Reflection Questions

Why do you think Jeremiah and Habakkuk were so resistant to the callings placed upon them to be prophets?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

Where do you turn when the world you thought you knew and understood seems to fall apart around you?

New Testament Bonus

Read Matthew 26:36-40. Similar to the prophets, Jesus is unable to get the people to come alongside him. Why was it difficult for the disciples to follow Jesus lead?
