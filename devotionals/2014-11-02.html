Wisdom Literature: Psalms of Praise

Today's reading assignment is Psalms 8, 33, 104, and 145. As you read, pay attention to the confident, serene faith in God and God’s promises expressed in these verses.

Reflection Questions

What images are used to represent God?

How do these Psalms add a new depth to Israel’s relationship with God?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

What and how do you communicate to God when you are praising God?
