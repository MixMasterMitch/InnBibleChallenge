Week 5, Day 7

November 18, 2014

Hope for those in exile

Today's reading assignment is Jeremiah 29:1-23, 31:15-20, and Ezekiel 37. As you read, pay attention to the promise of hope, healing, and a future given by God in the midst of dire circumstances, living as foreigners in a foreign land.

Reflection Questions

Why do you think those in exile are given the instructions they are given? Why do they have to stay for a certain amount of time?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

When have you most held on to the future hope of restoration promised in Jesus?

New Testament Bonus

Read John 5:5-15. What does the healing of the man by the pool tell us about patience in healing?
