Week 1, Day 6

October 20, 2014

Moses the Deliverer

Today's reading assignment is Exodus 2, 3, 4. As you read, pay attention to the circumstances of Moses birth that allow him to be the leader the Israelites need.

Reflection Questions

Why is Moses so reluctant to lead, even with the power of God on his side?

Connection to Your Faith

When have you not followed through because you were intimidated by the idea of serving or leading others?

New Testament Bonus

Read Matthew 17:1-8. What is the significance of Moses appearing on a mountain with Jesus?
