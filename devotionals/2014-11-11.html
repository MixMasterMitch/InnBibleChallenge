November 11, 2014

Fall of Northern Kingdom (Israel)

Today's reading assignment is 2 Kings 17. As you read, pay attention to: All of the reasons listed that the people had fallen out of favor with God and the chaos that dominates the Assyrian invasion.

Reflection Questions

What was missing from the Israelites understanding of themselves as God’s blessing for the world?

Questions To Consider Everyday

  • In what ways does this story connect with the overall narrative of scripture as I know it?
  • What side of the character of God do I see displayed in this story?
  • How is the character of God I see lived out in Jesus' life/Where is Jesus in the story?
  • In what ways does this story connect with my story?

Connection to Your Faith

When does your faith in Jesus take a backseat to the other “gods” in your life?

New Testament Bonus

Read Romans 8:37-39. What reassurance do we have of never falling out of favor with God?
